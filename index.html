The Destination

Ujjain — one of the seven Moksh Puris.

Home to the Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga, the only south-facing of the twelve Jyotirlingas. The Bhasma Aarti at 4 AM is unlike any spiritual experience in India.

Our villas place you at the doorstep of Ujjain's most sacred experiences — and within easy reach of its quieter ones too.

  • Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga — 2 km · 6 minutes drive
  • Mahakal Lok corridor — 2.2 km · open until 10 PM
  • Kal Bhairav Temple — 5 km · 12 minutes
  • Mangalnath Mandir — 6 km · sunset views
  • Ram Ghat (Shipra River) — 4 km · evening aarti
  • Sandipani Ashram — 5 km · the gurukul of Lord Krishna
  • Omkareshwar Jyotirlinga — 78 km · day trip
