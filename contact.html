Frequently Asked

The questions most pilgrims ask.

How far are you from Mahakaleshwar Temple?

All five villas are within 2 km of Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga — about 6 minutes drive or 12 minutes walk. We provide a complimentary shuttle service for our in-house guests.

Can you help us book the Bhasma Aarti?

Yes. Our caretaker will guide you on the booking process the evening you arrive — whether online or at the temple counter — and the shuttle drops you at the gate by 3:30 AM for the 4 AM aarti.

Do you offer food / catering?

Each villa has a fully equipped modular kitchen, so most families cook themselves. We can also arrange a local cook for breakfast, lunch, or dinner — simple home-cooked thalis at very fair rates.

What is your cancellation policy?

For standard dates, free cancellation up to 7 days before arrival. For festival dates (Mahashivratri, Shravan Mondays, Kumbh) we are strict — full advance, non-refundable. Please WhatsApp us for the exact policy on your dates.

Do you accept international guests?

Absolutely — we have hosted families from the US, UK, Australia, Singapore, the UAE, and many other countries. Our caretakers speak English. We accept international card payments via Razorpay or platform payments via Booking.com / Airbnb.
